About VORN App

VORN is a gamified Telegram WebApp that lets users earn rewards by completing daily and social missions. It connects with Google APIs only to verify YouTube actions like likes, comments, and subscriptions. No personal data is stored or shared outside this process.

Learn more in our Privacy Policy.
